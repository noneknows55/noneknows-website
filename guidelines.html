Community Guidelines

Last updated: April 2026

NoneKnows is built on anonymity, honesty, and trust. These guidelines exist to keep our community safe and meaningful. Anonymity is a privilege — not a shield for harm. We take violations seriously.

✓

What NoneKnows is for

  • Sharing personal confessions, secrets, and experiences anonymously
  • Expressing feelings and thoughts you can't share elsewhere
  • Supporting others through empathetic reactions and comments
  • Building an honest, judgment-free community
  • Discussing difficult topics in a safe, anonymous environment
✕

1. No threats or harassment

Anonymity does not give you the right to threaten or harass others.

  • Threats of violence against any person or group
  • Targeted harassment of specific individuals
  • Bullying, intimidation, or repeated unwanted contact
  • Encouraging others to harass someone
✕

2. No hate speech

Content that dehumanizes people based on protected characteristics is not allowed.

  • Hate speech targeting race, ethnicity, or national origin
  • Hate speech targeting religion or belief
  • Hate speech targeting gender or sexual orientation
  • Hate speech targeting disability or age
  • Use of slurs to attack people
✕

3. No self-harm promotion

We care about your wellbeing. Content that promotes or glorifies self-harm is strictly prohibited.

  • Encouraging suicide or self-harm in others
  • Sharing methods of self-harm
  • Glorifying eating disorders
  • Content designed to trigger vulnerable people

If you or someone you know is struggling, please reach out for help.
US: Call or text 988
International: findahelpline.com

✕

4. No sexual content involving minors

This is an absolute zero-tolerance rule. Any content that sexually exploits or endangers minors will result in immediate account termination and will be reported to the appropriate authorities including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC).

✕

5. No doxxing or privacy violations

Respect everyone's privacy — including people you know in real life.

  • Sharing someone's real name, address, phone, or email without consent
  • Revealing someone's location or workplace to harm them
  • Sharing private images or information without consent
  • Attempting to identify other anonymous NoneKnows users
✕

6. No illegal content

  • Content that facilitates or promotes illegal activity
  • Buying, selling, or trading illegal goods or services
  • Content that violates copyright or intellectual property law
  • Fraud, scams, or deceptive content
✕

7. No spam or manipulation

  • Repetitive posts or spam
  • Coordinated mass reporting to silence users
  • Creating multiple accounts to evade bans
  • Using bots or automated tools
  • Advertising or commercial promotion

⚖️ Consequences of violations

STEP 1 Content removal — violating posts are removed without notice
STEP 2 Auto-hide — posts with 5+ reports are automatically hidden from the community
STEP 3 Account termination — repeated violations result in permanent ban
STEP 4 Law enforcement referral — illegal content (especially CSAM) is reported to authorities immediately

🚩 How to report violations

See something that violates these guidelines? Tap the 🚩 flag icon on any confession in the app. Select the reason that best describes the violation. We review all reports and take action within 48 hours for serious violations.

For urgent safety concerns: support@noneknows.com
