Policy

Child Safety Standards

Last updated: April 2026

Our Commitment

NoneKnows has a zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to providing a safe environment and preventing any content or behavior that endangers minors.

Age Requirement

NoneKnows is intended for users aged 18 and older. An age verification gate is presented to all users upon first launch. Users who do not confirm they are of age cannot access the app.

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on NoneKnows:

  • Any content that sexually exploits or abuses minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors in any form
  • Sharing, distributing, or linking to CSAE material

Content Moderation

NoneKnows employs automated content filtering to block prohibited content at the point of submission. Users can report any content that violates our policies. Reported content is reviewed and actioned promptly. Accounts found violating these standards are permanently banned.

Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior that you believe constitutes child sexual abuse or exploitation, please report it immediately to support@noneknows.com.

We also report confirmed CSAE content to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and cooperate fully with law enforcement.

Contact

For any questions regarding our child safety standards, contact us at support@noneknows.com.
